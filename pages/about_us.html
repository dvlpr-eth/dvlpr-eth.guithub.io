The Humber College Transmedia Fellowship brings together students from across multiple programs in a collaborative effort to create an interactive multimedia storytelling project.
The Fellowship reflects the commitment of the School of Media Studies & Information Technology to provide students with innovative learning opportunities to prepare them for the challenges of storytelling in a rapidly changing digital landscape.
Managed by faculty, the core team of the Fellowship develops the concepts and content for the project, and additional Humber students then join to execute the vision.
The work you see here was completed in fourteen weeks, from seed-of-a-thought to user testing and launch.
If you have feedback or questions, we would love to hear from you: transmediahumber@gmail.com. This year’s team of fellows came together to tell stories of living a healthy life.
