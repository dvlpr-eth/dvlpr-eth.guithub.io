Walk a stop or two for fitness sake

The average distance between Toronto Transit Commission bus and streetcar stops is approximately 250 metres. Note: It’s recommended you have about 30 minutes of exercise every day.

Getting off a couple stops early on your commute burns calories and is a quick and easy way to get some exercise.
