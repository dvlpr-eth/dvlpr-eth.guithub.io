Mo-Cap Skeleton

This video was created using Qualysis, Humber’s state-of-the-art motion capture system

Using digital markers attached to the runner, students create a wire frame of a moving person. Humber’s Fitness and Exercise Science Lab uses the system to study sports biomechanics and gait. Their analysis of the data gathered can then be used to make corrections to prevent injury and optimize performance. The Lab’s motion-capture system is just one of the cutting-edge tools Humber offers aspiring Fitness and Health professionals.
