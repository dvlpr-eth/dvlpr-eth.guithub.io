Healthy locations

Wherever you are there is always an opportunity to be in nature.

We visited five conservation areas in the Greater Toronto Area to talk about how these places have influenced and inspired mindful living.

Hilton Falls

The Hilton Falls have a lot to offer any adventurous soul: a tranquil reservoir, rivers, and the breathtaking falls. There are also three bike-only trails that range from novice level to challenging hidden within the conservation area.
